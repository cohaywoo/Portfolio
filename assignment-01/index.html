Top 5 Minecraft Things

A semantic HTML list for Assignment 01.

  1. 1. Survival Mode

    The classic Minecraft experience — gathering resources, building shelters, and surviving mobs creates lasting memories and creative problem solving.

  2. 2. Redstone Contraptions

    Redstone allows for engineering solo or multiplayer machines like farms, doors, timers, and even simple computers.

  3. 3. Major Builds

    Constructing large-scale builds (castles, cities, pixel art) is a deeply satisfying creative outlet and a great showcase of skill.

  4. 4. Exploring Biomes & Structures

    Discovering villages, strongholds, temples, and new biomes fuels adventure and supplies rare loot and lore.

  5. 5. Multiplayer Servers

    Playing with friends on community servers—minigames, economies, and collaborative projects—keeps Minecraft endlessly replayable.
